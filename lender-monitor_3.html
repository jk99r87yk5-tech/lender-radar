How this works: Companies House lookup gives structured data — directors, PSCs, charges, filing history — free, for any UK company. Accounts 📑 fetches the latest filed accounts as iXBRL (machine-tagged) and auto-extracts the balance sheet: net assets, fixed assets, current assets, cash, debtors, stock — then computes NCAV and the 66% Graham threshold. Filings 📄 now has category filters (accounts / conf statements / officers / capital / charges) with one-click PDF for every filing. Confirmation statements are scanned PDFs with no structured data, so those open as PDF; shareholder control is better read from the PSC register anyway.
